# Competitor Analysis — AI Leadership Training for HR Professionals (UK Market, 2025-2026)

**Research method:** Deep multi-source research across 22 sources, 107 claims extracted, 25 adversarially verified (3-vote system), 17 confirmed, 8 killed. Conducted June 2026.

**Bottom line:** The market is fragmented across five tiers. **No confirmed competitor combines strategic AI leadership content specifically for CHROs with practical enterprise tool training such as Microsoft Copilot.** This is the gap Enlightened People's Strategic AI Leadership for HR workshop targets.

---

| Tier | Price Range | Examples | What They Offer | What They Miss |
|------|------------|----------|-----------------|----------------|
| **Premium conferences** | EUR 3,850-4,400 | Gartner HR Symposium | Executive networking, analyst insight, CHRO Circle | No practical skills, no deliverables, no follow-up |
| **University accelerators** | GBP 7,245+ | LSE AI Leadership | Academic credential, strategic frameworks | Not HR-specific, no tool training, 6-month commitment |
| **Mid-range specialist workshops** | GBP 1,035 / SGD 1,795-2,595 | CIPD, HRD Future | Facilitator-led, structured curriculum | CIPD targets mid-career; HRD Future is Asia-based |

| **Emerging certifications** | TBC (12 weeks) | Josh Bersin GHRE | Cohort-based, university-backed, AI-integrated | Proprietary platform (Galileo), 12-week commitment, not tool-specific |

**Our position:** GBP 6,000-8,000 in-house / GBP 750-950 per person open cohort — sits between specialist workshops and premium conferences, offering the strategic depth of the latter with the practical application of the former.

## The Confirmed Gap

**No verified competitor combines all three of:**

1. **Senior HR leader positioning** (CHROs, HR Directors, Heads of People) — CIPD targets mid-career; AIHR is individual/career-focused; LSE is cross-sector
2. **Strategic AI frameworks** (workforce architecture, governance, ROI, culture change) — tool training providers skip this; conferences cover it superficially
3. **Practical enterprise tool training** (Microsoft 365 Copilot) — conferences and certifications do not teach tools; tool trainers do not teach strategy

This is the niche the Strategic AI Leadership for HR workshop occupies.

## Recommendations

1. **Position against CIPD explicitly.** They are the default choice for UK HR professionals. Our message: "CIPD teaches mid-career professionals how to think about AI. We teach HR leaders how to lead it — with the tools, the frameworks, and the plan."

2. **Monitor Josh Bersin GHRE closely.** They are the closest strategic competitor and have a Microsoft Copilot partnership. Their 12-week format is a weakness we can exploit ("2 days, not 12 weeks"), but their content depth and brand will be strong.

3. **Use the Gartner 8% stat in every sales conversation.** It validates the skills gap and justifies the investment.

4. **Watch HRD Future's UK expansion.** Their 2-day format and "Copilot Edition" variant could become a direct competitor if they enter the UK market.

5. **Do not compete on credentials.** AIHR, Josh Bersin, and LSE all offer certifications. Our value is in the artefacts (the 5 deliverables), the facilitation quality, and the post-workshop follow-through — not a certificate.
