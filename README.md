## What This Is

This repository contains the complete course content, facilitator resources, and research for the **Strategic AI Leadership for HR** workshop — a 2-day programme designed for HR Directors, CHROs, People VPs, and senior HR professionals who need to lead AI adoption across their organisations.

**This is not another Copilot course.** It is not another AI awareness talk. It is the strategic layer that sits between board-level AI strategy and team-level tool training — the part almost nobody is delivering well.

## The Problem This Solves

HR leaders face a specific bind:
- **Conferences** give them inspiration and frameworks but no practical skills
- **Tool training** gives them Copilot clicks but no strategic context
- **Vendor pitches** give them product demos dressed as thought leadership
- **Certifications** give them credentials but not the judgement to deploy AI responsibly in a people function

This workshop gives them all four — strategy, practical skills, vendor-neutral insight, and a 90-day plan they leave with.
