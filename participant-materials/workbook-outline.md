# Participant Workbook — Strategic AI Leadership for HR

## How to Use This Workbook

This workbook is yours. It is not a handout to file — it is a working document. Every module has space for notes, every exercise has a template, and by the end of Day 2 you will have completed five deliverables you can take back to your organisation and use immediately.

---

## Your Five Deliverables

By the end of this workshop, you will have completed:

1. **AI Reality Check** — a scored assessment of where your organisation really stands
2. **Architecture Audit** — one process mapped across human work, machine work, and decision layers
3. **One-Page Governance Framework** — a practical, followable governance approach
4. **Board-Ready ROI Slide** — a business case your board can act on
5. **90-Day AI Leadership Plan** — named actions, named owners, named dates
