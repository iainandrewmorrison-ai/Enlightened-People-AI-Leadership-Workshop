# Market Positioning and Pricing — Strategic AI Leadership for HR

## Positioning Statement

**For** senior HR professionals who need to lead AI adoption across their organisations,
**Enlightened People's Strategic AI Leadership for HR** is a **2-day intensive workshop**
**that** bridges the gap between high-level AI strategy conferences and hands-on tool training,
**unlike** pure conferences (inspiration without skills), tool training (clicks without strategy), vendor events (pitches dressed as insight), and online certifications (credentials without judgement),
**because** it combines strategic frameworks with practical application, vendor-neutral insight with real-world case studies, and ends with a 90-day plan participants actually use.

---

## The Market Gap

The AI-for-HR training market has four dominant categories. Each has a specific weakness:

| Category | Examples | Strength | Weakness |
|----------|----------|----------|----------|
| **Conferences** | AI Experience Live, Gartner ReimagineHR, Unleash, HR Tech | Inspiration, networking, big-name speakers | No practical skills, no deliverables, no follow-up |
| **Online certifications** | AIHR, Josh Bersin Academy, Hacking HR, CIPD | Structured learning, credential, self-paced | No hands-on practice, no organisational context, completion rates low |
| **Vendor training** | Microsoft Copilot training, Workday Academy, SAP SuccessFactors | Deep tool knowledge | Single-vendor bias, no strategic context |
| **Consulting engagements** | Deloitte, PwC, McKinsey, Mercer | Bespoke, strategic, data-driven | Expensive (£50k-500k+), slow, creates dependency |

**Our position:** We operate in the space between all four — strategic enough to replace a conference, practical enough to replace tool training, independent enough to replace vendor events, and affordable enough to replace a consulting engagement.

## Differentiation — The Five Things Nobody Else Does Together

1. **Strategic + Practical in one programme.** Conferences give strategy. Training gives tools. We do both in two days.
2. **Five deliverables, not just learning.** Participants leave with a scored self-assessment, architecture audit, governance framework, ROI model, and 90-day plan.
3. **Vendor-neutral, practitioner-led.** No vendor sponsorship, no product pitches. Case studies from real organisations (Wrike, Vattenfall, Imperial Brands, NTT Data, 2 Sisters).
4. **Post-workshop follow-through.** Three check-in sessions over 90 days. Not a one-off event — a behaviour change programme.
5. **HR domain expertise.** The facilitator understands HR operations, not just AI technology. The exercises are HR-specific, the language is people-function language, the challenges are HR challenges.

| **Virtual** | 3 x half-days | Up to 16 | £5,000-6,500 | £310-400/person |

### Pricing Rationale

- **Conferences:** £500-1,500/person for 1 day (AI Experience Live, Unleash, HR Tech). No deliverables, no follow-up.
- **AIHR certificate:** ~$400-1,100 online. Self-paced, no facilitation, no organisational context.
- **Copilot training (tool-only):** £200-500/person for 1 day. No strategic context.
- **Our position:** Premium to tool training, comparable to conferences, fraction of consulting. Justified by deliverables and follow-through.

## Sales Approach

### Ideal First Conversation Starters

1. "Has your HR team figured out what AI is actually worth to the function — in numbers your board would believe?"
2. "What's your plan for when agents start doing HR tasks without anyone asking?"
3. "Most AI training teaches people how to click buttons. What's your plan for the strategic decisions that come before and after the clicks?"

### Objection Handling

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "We already have Copilot training" | "That's the tool layer. This is the strategy layer that makes the tool investment pay off. The two complement each other." |
| "We can't take senior people out for 2 days" | "They leave with a 90-day plan, a board-ready ROI case, and a governance framework. The alternative is 6 months of figuring it out piecemeal." |
| "We're waiting to see how AI settles" | "AI capability is doubling every 5 months. Every quarter you wait, the gap between you and your competitors widens." |
| "We have a consulting firm for this" | "A consulting engagement costs 10-50x more and takes 3-6 months. This gives your team the capability to lead it themselves in 2 days." |
| "Can we do it in 1 day?" | "Yes, as a condensed version. You lose depth on the honesty conversation and the planning time. But it works as an executive briefing." |
